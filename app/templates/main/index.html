What is Prokopa

Proko is a site where projects and students come together. The greatest advantage of Proko is that it helps to decrease everyone's workload: students can find projects to help with their studies, companies find people to do their projects and planning courses becomes easier for teachers. You don't need to be a student to use Proko. Everyone can join and create or sign up to do projects created by other people or companies.
